About Me

Front-End Dev

Hi, I am Brenda Michelle. Current front-end developer from California living in Belgium. I spend my time on personal projects and learning more about Front-end and how to improve my skills. I am passionate about building accessible, user-friendly websites and web applications. My skills include HTML, CSS(SASS), Javascript (ES6), React, GIT, Github, Netlify. However, I am constantly learning and am open to new technologies. If you think I would be a great fit for your team feel free to contact me so we can chat.

As a self-taught developer I am a fast, effecient and resourceful learner. With my abilities and skills I am able to solve problems quickly. In my free time I enjoy spending time with my family in nature, reading fiction, and making CSS art. Since I am actually very bad at drawing or painting CSS is my creative outlet.
